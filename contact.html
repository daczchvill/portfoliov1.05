ABOUT ME:

I’m a multidisciplinary visual artist with a deep passion for animation, digital storytelling, and creative web design. From character animation to motion graphics and HTML/CSS websites, I blend technical skill with artistic sensitivity to bring ideas to life. I have experience working across several creative fields — from 2D short films and web content to branding and audio-visual production. I enjoy styles that feel raw, honest, almost childlike — where hand-drawn textures, cutout animation, and imperfections bring charm and emotion to every frame. Tools I work with daily include Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and HTML/CSS. I’ve also collaborated on sound editing, storytelling, and graphic design pieces for various projects. My current focus is on building a career that bridges visual expression and storytelling, particularly inspired by the Scandinavian animation scene. Always open to new creative collaborations — feel free to reach out or explore some of my work below.
